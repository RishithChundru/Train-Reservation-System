CANCELLATION POLICY

1.This is for the passengers who has booked through e-portal.
2.For cancellation of tickets before 48hrs will be charged 20% as cancellation charges.
3.For cancellation of tickets between 24hrs to 48hrs will be charged 40% as cancellation charges.
4.For cancellation of tickets after 24 hrs and before preparation of chart will be charged 50% as cancellation charges.
5.For cancellation of tickets after preparation of chart, no refund will be entertained.
6.It is the passenger's responsibility to check which journey he/she was cancelling, no return of ticket will be entertained once the ticket got cancelled at any means.
7.By checking the checkbox it is confirmed that your were cancelled the ticket on your own.
