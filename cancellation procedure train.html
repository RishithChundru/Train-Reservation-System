CANCELLATION PROCEDURE

1.Enter PNR Number in the designated column.
2.PNR Number will be mentioned in the confirmed ticket.
3.Enter Train Number in the designated column.
4.Train Number will be mentioned in the confirmed ticket.
5.After entering the requested information. check the checkbox below which states that you have accepted to the cancellation policy.
6.After successful completion of the mentioned things click on Cancel button given below.
7.After successful cancellation of the ticket, an SMS that contains message as 'PNR xxxxxx1214's journey was successfully cancelled and the amount will be refunded within 3-4.
Incase of any discrepancy please contact to the helpdesk provided.' will be sent to the provided mobile number which was given by the passenger during the time of booking.
