CANCELLATION POLICY

1.Passenger is advised to cancel his/her tickets on/before 24 hours.
2.For cancellation of tickets before 48 hours,30% of the flight charges are charged as cancellation charges.
3.For cancellation of tickets after 48 hours and before 24 hours,50% of the flight charges are charged as cancellation charges.
4.Passenger is liable to his/her cancellation of bookings.
5.There will be no re-issueing of ticket after cancellation.
6.You'll be notified with a message that the booking was cancelled successfully.
7.The amount excluding cancellation charges will be refunded within 3-4 days.
8.In case of any discrepancy please contact to our helpdesk
